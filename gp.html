Our GP Services

Everything you need for your family's health — all in one place.

Primary Care Consultations

Expert assessment and management of both acute and chronic conditions.

  • Fever, infections & acute illness
  • Diabetes & hypertension management
  • Asthma & respiratory conditions

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care and monitoring for long-term health conditions.

  • Regular monitoring & treatment review
  • Medication management & counselling
  • Patient education & support

Preventive Healthcare

Proactive health screenings and lifestyle guidance to keep you well.

  • Routine health check-ups & screenings
  • Diet & exercise counselling
  • Smoking cessation support

Women's Health Services

Dedicated care for women at every stage of life.

  • Antenatal & postnatal care
  • Hormonal & menstrual disorder management
  • Family planning & contraception

Pediatric Care

Gentle, expert care for your children's health and development.

  • Growth & development monitoring
  • Common childhood illness management
  • Immunisation guidance

Minor Procedures & First Aid

Quick, safe treatment for minor injuries and urgent care needs.

  • Wound care & suturing
  • Abscess incision & drainage
  • Emergency stabilisation & basic life support
