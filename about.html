About Us

Awafe Medical Centre —
Trusted Healthcare in Opp. Danube Show Room, Al Riffa, Dibba Fujaira.

Who We Are: Awafe Medical Centre is a modern, comprehensive healthcare facility located in Opp. Danube Show Room, Al Riffa, Dibba Fujaira. We are proud to serve thousands of patients and families across the region with high quality, personalised and compassionate care every single day.

What We Do: We offer two core specialties under one roof — General Dentistry led by Dr. Shahanas A, and General Practitioner (GP) services led by Dr. Ahsana Mohamedunni Mohamed Ashraf. From routine dental check-ups and smile makeovers to chronic disease management and women's health, we cover all your healthcare needs.

Our Commitment: We use advanced 2D, 3D and cone beam CT imaging technology to ensure accurate diagnosis. Every treatment plan is tailored to the individual patient. We believe in pain-free, modern techniques and a patient-first approach that puts your comfort and well-being above everything else.

Opening Hours: We are open every day, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM — including weekends and public holidays — so you can always access the care you need.
