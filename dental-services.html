Dental Services

Our Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care delivered with compassion and expertise by Dr. Shahanas A at Awafe Medical Centre.

Root Canal Treatment & Retreatments

Root canal treatment is performed to save a tooth that is badly infected or decayed. We carefully remove the infected pulp, clean and shape the root canals, and seal them to prevent reinfection.

Extractions

We perform safe and comfortable tooth extractions when a tooth is too damaged to be saved, using modern anaesthetic techniques for a pain-free experience.

Dentures — Removable and Fixed

Custom-made removable and fixed dentures to restore your smile, confidence, and ability to eat and speak with ease.

Crown and Bridges

Custom-crafted crowns protect damaged teeth while bridges replace missing ones — both designed for a natural appearance and long-lasting durability.

Aesthetic Treatments — Veneers & Smile Makeover

Thin custom veneers bonded to your teeth, combined with a full smile makeover using whitening and contouring to give you the smile you have always wanted.

Tooth Whitening

Professional in-clinic whitening removes stains and delivers noticeably brighter results in a single visit.

Tooth Cleaning and Polishing

Professional scaling and polishing removes plaque, tartar, and surface stains — keeping your gums healthy and your teeth smooth and clean.

Pediatric Dental Care

Gentle, child-friendly dental care in a warm environment — from routine check-ups to early orthodontic assessments.
