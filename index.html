About Us

Awafe Medical Centre — Trusted Healthcare in Al Fujairah, UAE.

Awafe Medical Centre is a comprehensive healthcare facility in Al Fujairah, UAE. We provide high quality, personalised and compassionate care to our patients and are proud to have earned the trust of thousands of families across the region.

Our centre offers a full range of dental and general practitioner services — from routine check-ups and preventive care to advanced cosmetic dentistry, chronic disease management and women's health services.

Whether it is providing comprehensive dental care or primary medical services, Awafe Medical Centre is committed to delivering high quality, personalised care that matters — open every day, 9AM to 9PM.
