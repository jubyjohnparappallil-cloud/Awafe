Comprehensive Medical Care

Complete healthcare services for individuals and families.

1. Primary Care Consultations

  • Assessment and management of acute illnesses (e.g., infections, fever, pain)
  • Evaluation of chronic diseases (e.g., diabetes, hypertension, asthma)

2. Preventive Healthcare

  • Routine health check-ups and screenings
  • Lifestyle counseling (diet, exercise, smoking cessation)

3. Chronic Disease Management

  • Ongoing monitoring and treatment adjustments
  • Patient education and compliance support

4. Minor Procedures

  • Wound care and suturing
  • Incision and drainage of abscesses

5. Women's Health Services

  • Antenatal and postnatal care (basic level)
  • Menstrual and hormonal disorder management (basic level)
  • Family planning and contraception counseling

6. Pediatric Care

  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Management of common childhood illnesses
  • Immunization guidance

7. Emergency & First Aid Care

  • Initial stabilization of medical emergencies
  • Basic life support and urgent care management

8. Diagnostic Services

  • Ordering and interpreting laboratory tests
  • Basic ECG interpretation and other point-of-care diagnostics

9. Patient Education & Counseling

  • Health awareness and disease prevention
  • Medication counseling and adherence support
  • Mental health screening and basic support
