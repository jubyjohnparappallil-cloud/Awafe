GP Services

Our GP Services

Comprehensive primary healthcare for individuals and families — delivered with compassion by Dr. Ahsana Mohamedunni Mohamed Ashraf.

Primary Care Consultations

Assessment and management of acute illnesses such as infections, fever and pain. Evaluation of chronic diseases including diabetes, hypertension and asthma. We provide same-day appointments for urgent health concerns.

Preventive Healthcare

Routine health check-ups and screenings to detect issues early. Lifestyle counselling covering diet, exercise and smoking cessation to help you maintain a healthier life.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing monitoring and treatment adjustments for long-term conditions. Patient education and compliance support to help you manage your health effectively day to day.

Minor Procedures

Wound care and suturing for minor injuries. Incision and drainage of abscesses performed safely and efficiently in our clinic.

Women's Health Services

Antenatal and postnatal care at a basic level. Menstrual and hormonal disorder management. Family planning and contraception counselling for women at every stage of life.

Pediatric Care

Growth and development monitoring for children. Management of common childhood illnesses. Immunisation guidance and child health advice to support healthy development.

Emergency & First Aid Care

Initial stabilisation of medical emergencies. Basic life support and urgent care management to provide immediate assistance when it matters most.

Diagnostic Services

Ordering and interpreting laboratory tests. Basic ECG interpretation and other point-of-care diagnostics to support accurate and timely diagnosis.

Patient Education & Counseling

Health awareness and disease prevention guidance. Medication counselling and adherence support. Mental health screening and basic support for overall well-being.
